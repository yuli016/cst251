Aggretsuko

Aggretsuko may sound familiar to those who actively use Netflix. This is a Netflix Original anime that was initially released 2016 but is ongoing to this day. Aggretsuko got its most recent season added in August of 2020 and is planning for another.

The show is based in modern day Japan, where all of the people in the world are different types of animals. The show is about a red panda, Retsuko, who works in a dead end office job managed by a horrible boss. It focuses on how this character changes and evolves in her day to day life. Although it sounds somewhat drab, especially in these times, the show is actually a comedy. Aggretsuko is a delightful surprise, even if one isn't an avid anime watcher. It provides a feel good experience in these depressing times. Aggretsuko is definitely worth checking out next time Netflix feels dry.

-Vanessa Garcia
