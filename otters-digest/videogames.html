Video Games Recomendations

Playing video games is a popular hobby amongst many, escpecially for college students. Gaming has become more popular with the quarentining, so here are some of our recomendations.

There are a plethora of video games that one can choose from to satisfy any experience. If one desires a relaxing, laid back experience, we would recommend Animal Crossing: New Horizens. If one wanted to be a spectator, there are many competitive scenes to choose from. We would recommend looking into Counter Strike: Go’s competitive scene. If one wanted to experience an RPG with space shooter elements, then we would recommend looking into Destiny 2.
